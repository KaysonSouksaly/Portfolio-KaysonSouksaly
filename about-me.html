About Me

Why I chose to be a programmer

I am a student at Georgian College in Barrie, Ontario, with a strong passion for programming, particularly in web development and design. Growing up surrounded by technology and computers, I found my inspiration and drive in my father's career as a programmer. Many of my family members have also pursued roles in the tech industry, further fueling my interest and commitment to this field.

In my free time, I enjoy cooking, gardening, and dabbling in amateur carpentry. These hobbies provide me with a creative outlet that helps develop patience and attention to detail. I pride myself on my ability to work well in teams and my adaptability and flexibility in various situations make me a reliable and effective team member.
